It's one small step for us...

As we have been expanding our area of work, we left behind some valuable acknowledgements. Starting with a small group of four people, today we count more than 5000 volunteers envolved. Some of them are with us from the very begining, and others are yet to become the part of our team. By the end of 2011, one year after our foundation, we received The Green World Award for Environmental Best Practice by Companies, Organisations and Governments, since we had done a lot of field practices in a shot amount of time. In 2012 we opened the door for our first donating events which led to our forst cooperations with different environmental organisations such are The Ocean Cleanup, Green Deal, SavetheEarth etc. The most important thing for our team is to achieve people's attention and trigger them to act. If we want to make this world a better place to live, we should take a matter into our own hands and save our home. That's one small step for Globe.ly, one giant leap for mankind.
