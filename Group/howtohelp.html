Ways to help

Besides taking things into our own hands and doing everything that is in our power to stop climate changes and damages that harm us and the environment, Globe.ly workers are aware of the importance for people being informed well about these happenings. This is why we often organise presentations and events which should raise awareness about the most recent statistics of our endangering.

It is very important to know the ways of helping the Mother Nature. Since it is not possible always for everyone to participate in these events, or to listen to our presentations, we are happy to provide you with just a few ways you can help. This might mean a little to you, but it means a lot to our planet, so consider taking a couple of minutes to find out helpful tips.

One of the biggest problems that we face is pollution. To be precise, the level of pollution of world’s oceans is increasing. People ought to think that the level of the water is decreasing, but the fact is, pollution rises. Water on Earth is constant, but pollution is not. Ways to prevent water pollution would be keeping oils, fat or grease from the sink, minimal use of detergents or bleach, and of course avoiding direct dumping rubbish into water systems. Not only it pollutes water, but it harms living creatures that come in touch with that kind of disposal. It is well known that sea turtles are on the edge of extinction due to human actions and lifestyle.

Try not to use plastic carrier bags, which are not biodegradable nor recyclable. The problem is the fact that bags often end up polluting the ocean and infiltrating food sources. Use a reusable bag instead. When talking about recycling, it is important to know how to separate your waste. Try separating paper, glass, plastic and biodegradable waste. Make sure to even recycle your old newspapers, since recycling only one time per week could save a few thousands of trees. In fact, plant your own tree as well. It is really good for the environment; both the land and the air. All these small things could make a very big change in our lives.

Don’t forget the most important thing of all – share the knowledge! People are damaging the environment either because they do not know the facts, or have not yet consider a particular course of action. Share the facts to your friends and if they share it too, it could lead to the mass chain reaction whose effect could be immense. This is what Globe.ly tends to achieve.
