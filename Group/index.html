Welcome to Globe.ly

Globe.ly is a non-governmental organization whose purpose is to encourage people to join in saving the planet from ecological changes and uncleanliness it might face. It was founded in 2010 in Croatia, a country well known for its beautiful nature and scenery. While many different ecosystems are quickly falling apart, the people around the world are neglecting it and not truly processing what this means to them. Wild life are losing their homes and we are not far from that; that is why we need to act immediately to secure our planet from any ecological danger it might face. This is where Globe.ly comes in. People can now help through Globe.ly, either by volunteering in different cleaning and educational events or by donating to help Globe.ly go through with those events. Join us in helping the world!

Our mission

The founders have made it their mission to do something worthwhile that will help the world to continue showing it's nature and prosperity. We want to firstly encourage people to join our cause, either by actively participating in our different events, or donating. This helpes ensure a sense of community, a place where people can share their hopes of a better world with less junk around beaches and other different environments. One of our goals is also to educate people in small ways they can help the environment on their own. We achieve this by holding different presentations around the world where people get to come and find out ways they can easily help, without the need to volunteer. On this web site, we also ensured that the How To Help has information on different ways to help the environment.

Mate Ujčić

The leader of Globe.ly. He comes from Rovinj, Croatia and ever since he was young he was drawn to the sea. He decided that he would do everything in his power to keep the beauty of the beaches and recruited three of his friends and found Globe.ly, now one of the most communal organizations in the world.

Sara Hrg

The co-leader of Globe.ly. She comes from Slavonski Brod, Croatia. Because of her love of animals, she has decided to join Globe.ly in keeping the ecosystems stable and healty. She goes through the volunteers who have decided to help in Globe.ly events and gives them feedback on where and how they can help Globe.ly.

Pjeter Zimaj

The event handler. He comes from Zadar, Croatia and is of Albanian descent. When he saw the trash around the Albanian beaches, he decided he has to do something about it. He organizes the events and where they are handled. The events are usually done in places where there is high environmental danger.

Lara Modrić

The finance handler. She comes from Varazdin, Croatia. Because of her love nature and greenery, she decided to join Globe.ly. She is in charge of the finance. She takes the donations and incorporates them in the events. Globe.ly uses the donations for cleaning supplies and awarding the volunteers for participating.
